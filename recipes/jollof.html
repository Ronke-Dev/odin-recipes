Jollof rice recipe

The typical nigerian party food

This is a recipe i formulated a long time ago, and users should understand that the quality of the ingredient's products purchased actually matters.

Ingredients:

  • rice
  • chicken stock
  • curry
  • thyme
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • bay leaf
  • oil
  • sweet corn
  • tin tomato
  • fresh tomatoes
  • rodo
  • seasoning cubes

Steps:

  1. wash and Perboil your rice in the chicken stock
  2. take it off the stove when its quite soft(not too soft)
  3. pour some oil in an empty WOK pan and place it on the stove
  4. add in chopped onions and blended ginger & garlic
  5. add in your blended freshes tomatoes and rodo,let it fry for about 10minutes
  6. dilute tomato paste with some chicken stock and pour it into the wok pan
  7. add in your seasoning cubes, bay leaf,curry and thyme
  8. let it fry till its thick and quite dark
  9. then pour in your perboiled rice
  10. stir fry intermittently for 20minutes
  11. add in your sweet corn, stir and let it simmer for about 5 minutes
  12. voila,its ready

Eggnog waffles

Waffles are a delicious way to use up that leftover eggnog.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups eggnog
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Add eggnog, eggs, and butter; mix well.
  3. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Pour 1/4 of the batter into the preheated iron and cook until waffle is crisp and golden, about 5 minutes.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter.
